Cities

Oslo

Welcome to the new capital of Nordic cool! Get all our tips on top things to see and do in Oslo, including major attractions like MUNCH and the National Museum.

Bergen

Although it's the second-biggest city in Norway, Bergen is packed with small town charm.

Trondheim

The vibrant city of Trondheim is home to some of Trøndelag's most popular attractions, cosy shopping streets and a myriad of restaurants and bars offering local specialties.

Tromsø

From local food and mountains soaring above deep blue fjords to the midnight sun and the northern lights, discover the Arctic capital, Tromsø.

Stavanger

Breathtaking surroundings with fjords, mountains and long, white beaches. Stavanger and Sandnes also boasts an impressive assortment of museums and cultural events

Ålesund and Sunnmøre

Go hiking or skiing, admire Ålesund's unique Art Nouveau architecture, explore the fjords or just kick back and enjoy the view.

Bodø

Bodø is home to the world's strongest maelstrom, Saltstraumen. The largest city in Nordland is also just a boat ride away from the old trading post at Kjerringøy.
